Privacy Policy

Nevada Assisted Living ("we") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our website.

Information we collect

When you submit a form on this site (for example, the senior care matchmaker form), the form provider may collect the information you enter (such as name, phone, email, and care preferences). We do not store that information on our own servers. Use of embedded forms is also subject to the form provider's privacy policy.

How we use information

We use the site to provide general information about Nevada senior care. If you choose to submit a form, your information may be shared with our referral partners so they can contact you about care options. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing.

Cookies and tracking

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic operation (for example, to load embedded content). We do not use advertising or third-party tracking beyond what is needed for the forms and embeds we use.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or to request deletion of data you submitted via a form, contact the form provider used on the page where you submitted, or reach out via the contact method listed in our Disclosure page if we have added one.
