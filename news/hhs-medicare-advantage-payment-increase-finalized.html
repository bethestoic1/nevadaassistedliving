U.S. HHS Finalizes Medicare Advantage Payment Hikes for 2025-2026

2026-03-26 · local

The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) has finalized a significant increase in Medicare Advantage payment rates for the 2025-2026 cycle. This adjustment is expected to provide substantial financial relief to senior living providers across Nevada.

Focus on Clinical Outcomes

The new payment structure rewards facilities that achieve superior clinical outcomes in chronic condition management. For Nevada's assisted living network, this translates to higher reimbursements for on-site nursing care and preventative health measures.

Star Rating Adjustments

HHS is also introducing more stringent 'Star Rating' criteria that prioritize resident feedback and quality-of-life scores. Facilities that maintain a 4-star or higher rating will receive an additional 3.5% bonus, incentivizing a 'people-first' approach to elderly care.
