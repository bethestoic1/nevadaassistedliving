CMS Finalizes 2025 Medicare Advantage Payment Increases

2026-03-26 · nevada

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) has finalized its 2025 payment policies for Medicare Advantage and Part D plans. The new rules include a significant increase in payment rates, which is expected to improve the range of benefits offered to seniors enrolled in private plans.

Impact on Assisted Living

For assisted living operators, these payment hikes mean more robust reimbursement for clinical services provided within their facilities. This enables more providers to accept Medicare-linked plans while maintaining high ratios of staff-to-resident care.

New Star Rating Standards

The 2025 update also introduces stricter 'Star Rating' criteria, focusing more on patient outcomes and resident satisfaction. Facilities that achieve high ratings will be eligible for additional bonus payments, incentivizing a 'people-first' approach to elderly care.
