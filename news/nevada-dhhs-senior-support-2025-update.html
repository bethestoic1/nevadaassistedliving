Nevada DHHS Announces Enhanced Support Programs for Seniors

2026-03-26 · nevada

The Nevada Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS) has announced a new series of initiatives aimed at improving the quality of life for Nevada's aging population. These programs will focus on expanding access to high-quality assisted living and enhancing state-funded home care services.

New Resources for Families

The DHHS is launching a centralized portal to help families find licensed assisted living facilities that meet specific medical and financial criteria. This initiative is part of a broader state commitment to ensure that Nevada's seniors can age with dignity in safe, professional environments.

Funding and Grants

Significant additional funding has been allocated to grant programs that support the modernization of facility infrastructure, including improved medical monitoring systems and common-area renovations. These grants aim to keep Nevada's care standards among the highest in the Southwest.
