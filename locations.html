Where to Live: Southern vs. Northern Nevada

Choosing a location is the first step in the Nevada senior care journey.

Las Vegas & Henderson (Clark County)

Best for: Resort-style amenities and specialized medical networks. Access to the top cardiac and geriatric specialists in the Southwest.

Reno & Sparks (Washoe County)

Best for: Cooler climates and proximity to world-class respiratory care. Washoe County currently boasts some of the highest-rated nursing ratios in the state for 2026.
