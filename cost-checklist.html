Nevada Assisted Living Cost Checklist (2026)

Use this checklist when planning or comparing costs. You can print this page or share it with family. Always confirm figures with official state and facility sources.

Monthly cost ranges (2026)

  • Henderson: ~$3,650 (assisted living)
  • Nevada average: ~$3,750
  • National average: ~$4,500

Financial aid quick reference

  • Medicaid HCBS waiver (Nevada) income cap: $2,982/month (single applicant). Miller Trust may help if over the limit.
  • Senior tax deduction (federal): Up to $6,000 individual / $12,000 joint under the “One Big Beautiful Bill” Act.
  • VA Aid & Attendance: Up to about $2,400/month for eligible wartime veterans.

Before you choose a facility

  • Confirm 2026 pricing and any move-in specials.
  • Ask about Medicaid acceptance if you may need it later.
  • For memory care: ask about staff-to-resident ratios and 2026 state standards.
  • See our Henderson, Las Vegas, and Reno guides and Vegas vs. Reno for location-specific tips.
