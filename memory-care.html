Specialized Memory Care in Nevada

Memory care is more than just a secured wing; in 2026, Nevada has implemented new "Person-Centered" requirements for Alzheimer's and dementia facilities in Clark and Washoe counties. When you're comparing options—whether in Henderson, Las Vegas, or Reno—these are the standards and features that matter most.

What to Look for in 2026

  • Staff-to-Resident Ratios: New Nevada DHS benchmarks recommend at least 1:5 during daylight hours for memory care units. Ask each facility what they actually staff; ratios can vary by shift and occupancy.
  • Smart Monitoring: Many newer communities use AI fall-detection, wearable alerts, or biometric wandering systems. These don't replace human care but can improve safety and family peace of mind.
  • Circadian Lighting: High-end Southern Nevada facilities are adopting light therapy and tuned lighting to reduce "sundowning" and support sleep. Worth asking about on tours.
  • Structured Activities: Person-centered care means programming tailored to interests and abilities. Ask how the calendar is built and whether residents are grouped by cognitive stage where appropriate.

Memory Care Costs in Nevada (2026)

Memory care typically runs $1,000–$1,500 more per month than standard assisted living in the same area. In Henderson, where assisted living averages around $3,650, expect memory care in the mid-$4,000s and up. Las Vegas and Reno are similar. Funding options—Medicaid waiver (with strict income limits), VA Aid & Attendance, LTC insurance, and the 2026 senior tax deduction—apply to memory care as well; see our Financial Aid Guide and Cost Checklist.

FAQs About Memory Care in Nevada

Is memory care in Nevada licensed separately? Memory care is often provided within a licensed assisted living facility or a distinct memory care license. Nevada DHS sets standards for both; ask the facility about their specific certification and any recent surveys.

Where can I find memory care in Henderson or Las Vegas? Many Southern Nevada communities offer memory care in Green Valley Ranch, Summerlin, and other areas.
