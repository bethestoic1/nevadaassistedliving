Specialized Memory Care in Nevada

Memory care is more than just a secured wing; in 2026, Nevada has implemented new "Person-Centered" requirements for facility staffing.

What to Look for in 2026:

  • Staff-to-Resident Ratios: New state benchmarks recommend 1:5 during daylight hours.
  • Smart Monitoring: Does the facility use AI fall-detection or bio-metric wandering alerts?
  • Circadian Lighting: High-end Vegas facilities are now using light therapy to reduce "sundowning" behaviors.
